Information on demand (new!)

In this stage, all types of information from the AI algorithm will be available on demand.

As before, your task is to identify which of the listed conditions are present in the X-rays. If desired, you will be able to view any of the following pieces of information from the AI diagnostic tool for each condition in every image:

  • AI recommendation
  • AI confidence
  • AI focus
  • global explanations of failure.

For each case, you can obtain the AI's information
  • by using the toggle switch present in the navigation bar on the top of the page, for the AI's binary recommendations and
  • once the AI's binary recommendations have been activated, by clicking on the buttons present in each of the conditions in the cards of the different conditions.
