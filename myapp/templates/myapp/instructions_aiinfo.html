AI Diagnostic Tool

In all stages of the study, you will work alongside an artificial intelligence (AI) diagnostic tool. This AI diagnostic tool tool uses machine learning models that have been trained on X-rays with condition labels generated by a pool of expert radiologists.

The AI diagnostic tool's predictions

The AI diagnostic tool produces probability predictions for each of the 33 listed conditions. These predictions consist of numeric scores between 0% and 100% that indicate the likelihood that the condition is present in the X-ray, according to the AI diagnostic tool. A value of 100% indicates that the condition is certainly present in the image, according to the tool. A value of 0%, instead, means that the tool predicts that the condition is certainly not present in the X-ray. The probability predictions generated by the AI tool may not be well calibrated. This means that, for example, among all the images for which the tool predicts that some condition is present with probability 80%, the condition might actually be present only 60% of the times. It is common to transform these probability predictions into binary predictions. Here, we have added icons containing red triangles in the navigation bar on the right next to the conditions for which tool's estimated probability is larger than or equal to 60%.
