Justified Trust Study

Purpose

This study aims to understand how veterinarian radiologists work alongside artificial intelligence (AI) systems in diagnostic tasks. More specifically, we will investigate whether these AI diagnostic tools would be useful in a radiologist's daily decision-making.

Study Description

This study consists of 10 phases. In each of these phases, you will be asked to make diagnostic recommendations on 20 clinical cases using X-rays. Each phase will take between 30 minutes and 1 hour. In different phases, you will receive various types of assistance from an artificial intelligence (AI) system. During the study, you will also be asked to reflect on how useful various types of AI information were in your decision-making process.
